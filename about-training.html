Training

Entry Expectations

  • No prior experience required
  • Open to all fitness levels
  • Commitment to turning up and putting in the effort
  • Willingness to take hits and learn from them

Progression

  • Beginners start with fundamentals before full contact
  • Iintensity increases as soon as we deem you safe enough
  • Full contact once you are ready and equipped
  • Progression to events and competitive fighting

Drills

  • Basics - footwork, weapon handling and striking mechanics
  • Shield use and defensive positioning
  • Tactical drills — reading opponents and creating openings
  • The Eights, Bridge, Triangle, 1v1s, Laddger, etc...

Development

  • Physical conditioning alongside technical skill
  • Fighting fitness — endurance, strength, reaction time
  • Tactical awareness in both solo and group contexts
  • Mental composure under pressure
