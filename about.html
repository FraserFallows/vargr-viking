About Us

Step into the shield wall

Vargr Viking is a Suffolk-based tactical combat group dedicated to recreating the warrior traditions of the 9th to 11th centuries through full-contact historical combat. We take part in events across the UK and Europe, ranging from small-scale skirmishes to massive battles involving hundreds of fighters.

Our focus is on authentic combat, constant improvement, and representing the group with pride. Whether on the training ground or the battlefield, everything we do is built around pressure, teamwork, and pushing physical and tactical limits in a controlled environment.

More than just a combat group, Vargr Viking is a community built on loyalty, respect, and shared hardship. We train hard, compete hard, and support one another both on and off the field.

Whether your goal is competitive fighting, historical martial skill, or becoming part of a close-knit team, you’ll find a place here. We train at The Red Lion in Great Bricett, Suffolk, and welcome anyone interested in discovering what full-contact historical combat is really about.
