Combat

Eastern

  • Full contact with blunt steel weapons
  • Valid targets: head, shoulders, upper arms, body down to the knee
  • Suited to both duels and large-scale battles
  • Foundation style for all new fighters

Huscarl

  • Builds on Eastern with expanded target zones
  • Additional valid targets: forearms, hands, below the knee
  • Demands tighter guard and more precise footwork
  • More technically demanding, rewards disciplined fighters

Engagements

  • Skirmish: small group or one-on-one combat
  • Mass battle: hundreds of fighters per side
  • Events range from local UK meets to international competitions
  • We compete in both formats throughout the year

Shield Wall

  • Coordinated defensive and offensive formation
  • Requires trust and communication between fighters
  • Positioning and timing are as important as individual skill
  • Core to mass battle strategy
