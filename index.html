About

Vargr Viking is a Suffolk-based tactical combat group, competing in Eastern and Huscarl events across the UK.

Eastern

Eastern permits strikes to the head, shoulders, upper arms and body down to the knee, making it well suited to both small skirmishes and mass battles hundreds a side.

Huscarl

Huscarl expands on Eastern with more valid target zones, including forearms, hands and below the knee, demanding a more dynamic and technical approach to both offence and defence.

Our Weapons

Eastern and Huscarl are both full contact styles fought with blunt steel weapons. We compete in both across events throughout the UK and Europe.
